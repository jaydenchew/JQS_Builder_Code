Step 1 of 3 — Capture Photo

Place the calibration card on the phone stage with its printed edges parallel to the arm's X/Y axes. Jog the arm so the camera sees the entire inner black square.

mm (default 50mm; check card's printed label)

Step 2 of 3 — Click Corners

Click the 4 corners of the inner black square in this order: TL → TR → BR → BL.

Jog the pen tip over the card crosshair, lower the pen with Press to verify it physically touches the crosshair center, then click Set Pen Reference. Arrow keys also work.
